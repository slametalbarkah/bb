Bath & Body Works, LLC. is an American retail store chain that sells soaps, lotions, fragrances, and candles. It was founded in 1990 in New Albany , Ohio and has since expanded across six continents. [ 2 ] In 1997, it became the largest bath shop chain in the United States. [ 3 ]

History

[ edit ]

Bath & Body Works was founded in 1990 in New Albany, Ohio as the beauty line for Express, Inc. [ 4 ] The company's first store opened in a Cambridge, Massachusetts mall in September 1990. [ 5 ] In 1991, the Body Shop sued Bath & Body Works, resulting in Bath & Body Works rebranding [ 6 ] The brand created a mascot named Kate who serves as a founding figure, with the store layout being modeled as her country house. [ 7 ] In 1997, a secondary brand known as Bath & Body Works at Home was launched. [ 3 ] Two years later, the company launched the White Barn Candle Company, a division specializing in the sale of scented candles . [ 8 ]

Bath & Body Works launched both a seasonal catalog and a website in 2006. In November 2006, the company launched its first television commercial advertisement. [ 9 ] Net sales as of January 28, 2006 were $2.3 billion, significantly higher than all other LÂ Brands companies other than Victoria's Secret . [ 10 ]

In July 2008, the company announced that it was opening six locations in Canada . With the company acquiring Canadian-based La Senza , they felt it was the opportunity to move into a growing Canadian market, with The Body Shop being its main competition. [ 11 ] [ 12 ]

Bath & Body Works operates more than 1,900 stores. [ 13 ] In October 2010, it opened its first stores outside of North America in Kuwait , by the franchise giant M.Â H. Alshaya Co. [ 14 ]
